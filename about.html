About Me.

I am born and raised in Western Pennsylvania. After graduating from Indiana Area Senior Highschool I am now a freshman at Penn State University (Main Campus) My areas of interest have always been math, science, and engineering. For all four years of high school I have been treasurer of my school's Technology Education Club and for two years our National History Day Club. Also, I am a member of my school's Cross Country, Indoor Track, and Outdoor Track teams. In my free time I read books and teach myself new things depending on what I am currently interested in. Right now, my focus has been 3D modeling and rendering using Blender.
